This is the history of FC Hwasung. Hwaseong Football Club (Hwaseong Football Club) is a Korean football team founded on January 23, 2013 based in Hwaseong-si, Gyeonggi-do, and is currently participating in the K3 League He won the K3 League for the first time in the 2014 season.

History of FC Hwasung. The club was founded on 23 January 2013 The team managed to finish top of their group in their first season, but lost to Pocheon FC in the playoff stage. In only their second year of competition the club again finished top of their group and progressed to the final where they defeated Pocheon FC to become the 2015 Champions..
